Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to the Awesome-Gamerz Discord Bot. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our bot.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Messages: We collect and store messages sent to and from the bot to improve its functionality and user experience.
  • User Data: We collect user IDs, usernames, and other relevant data to provide personalized services.
  • Interaction Data: We collect data on how users interact with the bot, including commands used and responses received.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To improve the functionality and performance of the bot.
  • To provide personalized services and responses.
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve user experience.
  • To communicate with users about updates and changes to the bot.

How We Protect Your Information

We take the following measures to protect your information:

  • We use secure servers and encryption to protect data in transit and at rest.
  • We restrict access to collected data to authorized personnel only.
  • We regularly review our data collection and storage practices to ensure data security.

Sharing Your Information

We do not share your information with third parties, except in the following circumstances:

  • When required by law or legal process.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or others.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • The right to access the information we hold about you.
  • The right to request the correction or deletion of your information.
  • The right to object to the processing of your information.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@awesome-gamerz.com.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@awesome-gamerz.com.
